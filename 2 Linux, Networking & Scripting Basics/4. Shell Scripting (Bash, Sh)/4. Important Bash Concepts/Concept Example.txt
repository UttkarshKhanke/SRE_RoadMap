| Concept | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **`&&` and `||`** | Chain commands: `command1 && command2` |
| **Pipes (`|`)** | Pass output: `ls | grep txt` |
| **Redirects (`>`, `>>`, `<`)** | Output/Input: `ls > files.txt` |
| **Arrays** | `arr=(one two three)` and `${arr[0]}` |
| **String Manipulation** | `${var#pattern}`, `${var%pattern}` |
| **Process Control** | Background jobs: `sleep 10 &` |
