🔁 SRE vs DevOps – Similarities

🔷 Aspect	✅ Similarities
Goal	Both aim to deliver software faster, reliably, and at scale.
Automation	Strong focus on automation to reduce manual work and errors.
Collaboration	Promote collaboration between dev and ops teams.
Monitoring & Observability	Emphasize metrics, logging, and proactive issue detection.
CI/CD	Both embrace Continuous Integration / Continuous Delivery practices.
Incident Management	Handle incidents systematically, learn from failures.
Cloud-Native Focus	Fit naturally into cloud-native and microservices environments.
